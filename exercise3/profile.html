Elina Garanca

Elina Garanca is a Latvian mezzo-soprano, born on September 16, 1976. She has appeared on the stage of the Metropolitan Opera over seventy times, earning critical acclaim. Among her most famous roles include the title character in Bizet's Carmen, Octavian in R. Strauss's Der Rosenkavalier, Angelina in Rossini's La Cenerentola, Dalila in Saint-Saens' Samson et Dalila, and Sesto in Mozart's La Clemenza di Tito. She has performed solo recitals at New York's Carnegie Hall, a tremendous honor for any singer. This year, she won the Female Singer of the Year for Germany's preeminent classical music award, the Opus Klassik, for her album Sol y Vida. Just this month, she released an album of songs by Brahms and Schumann, her latest in a series of albums with Deutsche Grammophon.
